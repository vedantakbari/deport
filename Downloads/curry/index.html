About Us

The Aussie WIRE Story

Learn more about our powerful script hub and the team behind it.

Aussie WIRE is a free script hub created by a dev formerly known as pcallskeleton (search his name to see his previous work). He has now rebranded and made a new hub that provides an enhanced gaming experience across multiple popular Roblox games.

Our commitment is to provide high-quality, regularly updated scripts that work flawlessly across all supported games. With advanced features and user-friendly interfaces, Aussie WIRE makes scripting accessible to everyone.
